Roadmap for Learning App Development

Follow these steps to become a proficient app developer!

  • 1. Learn the Basics

    HTML, CSS, JavaScript

  • 2. Choose a Platform

    Android (Java/Kotlin) or iOS (Swift)

  • 3. Explore Cross-Platform Development

    React Native, Flutter, or Xamarin

  • 4. Learn Backend Development

    Node.js, Django, Firebase

  • 5. Master App Deployment

    Google Play Store, Apple App Store
